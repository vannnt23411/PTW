Autumn Folk Festival

From September 10th–12th, come to Sunrise for the annual autumn folk festival... Camping spots are still available at Ohanapecosh.

In addition to the intimate songs... Call Ted Cashman (555-8129) to sign up for one of the workshops.

Visitors Prepare to Meet MRIM

Want to see what it’s like to hover over Columbia Crest at 14,400 feet... Try the Mount Rainier Interactive Map now available at the Paradise visitors’ center.

MRIM uses state-of-the-art computers... Contact Doug LeCourt at Paradise for more information.
